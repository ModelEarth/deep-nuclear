BYD-style flagship company flywheel

Flagship-firm prestigeInference: if a city helps scale a breakout EV or battery champion, the mayor can be seen as unusually effective at industrial strategy.
Promotion narrativeThe story becomes: this mayor did not just manage growth, but created a nationally significant sector winner.
Accolades and visibilityMore inspections, conference slots, policy-pilot status, model-city branding, and media attention can follow.
Cluster multiplierA flagship firm attracts suppliers, engineers, logistics, finance, and training institutions, making the achievement look repeatable.
Export credibilityStrong manufacturing exports and recognizable local champions reinforce the image of globally competitive governance.
Talent magnet effectCity leadership gains prestige when graduates, founders, and investors start treating the city as a serious destination.
